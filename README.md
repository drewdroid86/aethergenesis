## What Is ÆTHERGENESIS?

ÆTHERGENESIS is a real-time interactive universe simulator built on 
WebGL 2.0 and Three.js. It is not a game. It is not a screensaver. 
It is a scientifically grounded sandbox where you manipulate the 
fundamental constants that govern all of existence — and watch the 
consequences unfold across 13.8 billion years of cosmic time.

Change the gravitational constant and stars refuse to form.
Lower the fine-structure constant and chemistry becomes impossible.
Crank the cosmological constant and watch the universe tear itself apart.

This is what it feels like to be at the controls before the Big Bang.

## 🔬 The Real Science

ÆTHERGENESIS is built on peer-reviewed physics:

| Constant | Value Used | What It Controls |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|
| Hubble Constant H₀ | 67.4 km/s/Mpc | Universe expansion rate |
| Fine-structure constant α | 1/137 | Atomic bonding, chemistry |
| Gravitational constant G | 6.674×10⁻¹¹ | Star and galaxy formation |
| Cosmological constant Λ | 1.1×10⁻⁵² m⁻² | Dark energy, heat death |
| CMB Temperature | 2.725 K | Background radiation today |

**Rendering physics:**
- Schwarzschild metric for black hole spacetime curvature
- Initial Mass Function (Kroupa/Chabrier) for stellar distribution
- Logarithmic spiral mathematics for galaxy arm structure
- Barnes-Hut O(n log n) for N-body gravitational approximation
